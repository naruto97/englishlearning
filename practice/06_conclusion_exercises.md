## ANSWER KEY

### Exercise 1 Sample Restatements:

**Thesis A Restatement:**
> "The evidence confirms that a college degree delivers advantages its alternatives cannot match—financial returns, professional mobility, and adaptable expertise that serve graduates throughout their careers."

**Thesis B Restatement:**
> "When measured across a full career, higher education proves its value through substantially greater earnings, broader opportunities, and stronger resilience during economic uncertainty."

### Exercise 2 Answers:
- Conclusion A: **Copy** (exact same words)
- Conclusion B: **Restatement** (same meaning, different words)
- Conclusion C: **Copy** (adds "As stated in the introduction" but still copies)

### Exercise 3 Sample:
> "Higher lifetime earnings, broader career options, and advantages that persist even when trade work's initial appeal fades—all favor the college-educated worker."

### Exercise 4 Answers:
- Summary A missing: **Counterargument** (trade school limitations)
- Summary B missing: **Flexibility**
- Summary C missing: **Earnings**

### Exercise 7 Answers:
- A: Restatement (just states a fact)
- B: **Reflection** (adds insight about compounding)
- C: **Reflection** (acknowledges nuance)
- D: Restatement (just lists benefits)

### Exercise 10 Answers:
- A: Summary/reflection
- B: **New info** (new study not mentioned in body)
- C: Summary/reflection
- D: **New info** (networking not discussed in body)
- E: Summary/reflection

### Exercise 11:
**New info:** "College also offers valuable internship opportunities that help students build professional experience before graduating."

While trade school works for some, most students benefit more from higher education. High school seniors should view college as an investment in their future."
